\section{Contributions}

The paper makes five concrete contributions:

\begin{enumerate}
\item a rank-2 FlexField that extends single-heightfield slicing to multi-depth control,
\item a top-hit plus second-hit extraction module for shallow internal ceilings and terraces,
\item an anisotropy-aware least-squares objective module,
\item a warp / slice / unwarp integration path for mainstream slicers,
\item benchmark definitions and metrics that separate `K=1` and `K=2` capability.
\end{enumerate}
